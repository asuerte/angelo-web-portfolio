Overview

Project Overview

This is a demo project built around a fictional synthetic lawn installation business in Sydney. The brief I set for myself: build the kind of landing page that would actually work for a local tradie looking to generate leads from Google. That means fast load times, a strong above-the-fold hook, multiple entry points into the quote funnel, and the trust signals that make a homeowner feel confident enough to fill in a form.

The Brief

What I Was Solving For

Local service businesses often rely on just a Google listing and word of mouth. The first challenge was designing something that establishes credibility instantly.

The synthetic lawn niche is competitive in Sydney. The page needed clear differentiation by leading with proof points and benefits rather than generic service descriptions.

Most visitors from local search are on mobile, comparing a few options at once. The layout had to load fast and communicate value in the first few seconds before they bounce.

Without a clear conversion path, interested visitors leave without making contact. Every section needed a logical next step guiding the user toward requesting a quote.

The Approach

How I Built It

Hero with immediate proof. The above-the-fold section leads with a Google rating badge and review count, followed by a benefit-led headline. Two CTAs are visible without scrolling.

Trust bar below the fold. Four scannable proof points (200+ installs, pet & child safe, fully insured, 2-year guarantee) presented as a visual strip immediately after the hero to reinforce credibility fast.

Services with CTAs baked in. Each of the four service cards has its own "Get a Free Quote" link, so a visitor who knows what they want can convert directly from the services section without scrolling further.

Project gallery for visual confidence. A photo grid of completed lawns gives visitors something tangible to react to. Seeing the finished result makes it easier to commit to a quote request.

Google-style testimonials. Reviews are styled to mirror the look of Google reviews, triggering the same trust response people feel when reading them on a real business profile.

Local SEO foundations. Headings, meta tags, and body copy are structured around "synthetic lawn installation Sydney" and related terms, giving the page a solid base to rank from.

Sticky nav with phone CTA. Mobile visitors can tap to call from anywhere on the page. For local service businesses, a direct phone call is often the highest-converting action available.

"Why Us" section. A dedicated section handles the main objection homeowners have before committing ("why should I choose you?"), answered with three clear, benefit-driven points and a supporting image.
