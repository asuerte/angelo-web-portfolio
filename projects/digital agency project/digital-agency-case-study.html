Overview

Project Overview

This is the official website for Ascent Digital, my digital agency. I designed and built it entirely myself to serve as both a client-facing platform and a live demonstration of my development capabilities. The site needed to communicate what we do, why clients should trust us, and make it easy to get in contact.

The Challenge

The Problem

No existing platform to present services and demonstrate credibility to potential clients.

Needed to clearly differentiate the agency's offering in a competitive market.

No structured conversion flow to turn site visitors into client enquiries.

Required a design that itself serves as proof of the agency's quality and aesthetic standards.

The Approach

The Solution

Clean, modern UI designed for clarity, with every section having a clear purpose and message.

Dedicated service sections with detailed explanations of each offering.

Strong visual hierarchy guiding users from awareness through to enquiry.

Strategic CTA placement at multiple scroll points to capture interest at the right moment.

Performance-optimised build for fast load times on all devices.

SEO-friendly page structure to improve organic discoverability.
